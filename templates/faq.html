Frequently Asked Questions
What is Handmade?
Handmade is an ecommerce website designed to sell handmade goods. This ranges from selling artwork to watches, anything you make or create can be sold here.
What is Personalization?
A personalized account allows for users to be able to review and rate products as well as recieve recommendations on new products that are specific towards your interests.
How do I sell an item?
In order to sell an item you first must create an account and personalize your account. Once you have done so you can list your item using the sell button on the homepage to create a listing and sell your product.
